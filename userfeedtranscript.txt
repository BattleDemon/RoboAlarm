User Feedback

User testing showed that the overall concept of RoboAlarm works well, with most users finding it engaging and effective at forcing them to wake up. The challenge system was generally seen as fun and motivating, and users liked the interactive nature of the device compared to a normal alarm. However, the testing also revealed several usability and reliability issues that significantly affected the experience.

The most common issue was a lack of clear instructions during challenges. Users often did not understand what they were supposed to do or which sensor to use without being told. In addition to this, several technical problems were identified, including inconsistent sensor readings (especially the colour and gyro), challenges not resetting correctly, and the alarm not always turning off as expected. Some challenges, particularly the motor speed task, were also too difficult due to tight tolerances and high target values. The menu system was functional but felt clunky at times, with slow response and awkward navigation. Overall, the feedback suggests that the core idea is strong, but improvements are needed in clarity, difficulty balancing, and system stability.
